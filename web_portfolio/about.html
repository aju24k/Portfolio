About

My name is Anjo B. Baraquiel, a freelance working college student from Bicol University
Polangui, studying a multimedia course in information technology with a major in animation.
I work hard to turn the ordinary into extraordinary projects to make my clients satisfied.
I am highly flexible and adaptable when it comes to different projects and freelance work.
I was skilled using Adobe Photoshop software for making graphic designs, like photo
manipulation, painting, and making logos. Also other software that is common with Photoshop,
I have experience using Clip Studio Paint for making digital art, concept art, designing
characters, and also animation. For video editing and animation, I use Adobe Premier and
Adobe After Effects. I'm also skilled at making 3D models using Blender. For now, I'm using
a laptop to work on the projects and a pentab for my digital art and other skills.
